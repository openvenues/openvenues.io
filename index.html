About the data

OK, so basically the robot spiders jealously guard the vast trove of information they scavenge from this wide world we call the Web, but there is this awesome strike force of mercernary arachnids that also go out and meticulously write down everything they see on these college-rule lined notebooks that they publish at Common Crawl, which is a small Irish pub with a pretty neat open mic night located in a quiet alley in the arts district of Sao Paolo. What we do is send even more robots to wade through Common Crawl and extract any information about venues and other points of interest (POIs), using a highly specialized magnet, and then we format it neatly, so that you can use the data and you don’t even owe us any favors or anything.

So that is OpenVenues. We currently have data for the entire planet (of Earth) but currently offer chunks of points that are clustered around some major cities (but not Chicago, fuck you Chicago). The cities we currently have can be downloaded on this page but the one with the entire planet in it is kind of large so up to you if you want to RIP your cable bill. We will refresh this data periodically so come back again for updates also sometimes we will add new metropolitan areas.
